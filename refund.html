At GR & KDR Transport, we strive to deliver dependable and customer-friendly services. Our refund and cancellation policy is designed to ensure clarity and fairness for all users of our platform.

1. Cancellations by Customers

Customers may cancel a booking at any time before the driver reaches the pickup location. However, if the driver is already en route or has arrived, a nominal cancellation fee may be applied to compensate for time and fuel costs.

2. Refund Policy

In cases where a booking has been pre-paid and subsequently canceled, a refund will be processed after deducting any applicable cancellation charges. Refunds are typically issued within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

3. Driver-Initiated Cancellations

If a driver cancels a booking, customers will be notified promptly, and any pre-paid amount will be fully refunded without deductions.

4. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please contact our support team with your booking reference number and reason for cancellation. Our team will review your request and respond within 48 hours.
